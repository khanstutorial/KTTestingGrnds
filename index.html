Why do families choose Khan's?

Developed by Senior Curriculum leaders at KT, our curricula cover all aspects of the NY State Common Core and High School Curriculum as well as preparation for the SHSAT and SAT.

Subject Tutoring

We tutor our students in any areas where they need help; this includes homework, regular class exams, mid-terms & finals.

Standardized Tests

We cover all standardized testing needs. Our programs include prep for your NYS Common Core, SHSAT, Regents, AP, and SAT exams.

Our Program

KT's renowned leaders are regularly trained by Senior KT officials to provide the best quality to our families.

Focus on Our Community

We provide academic counseling and mentorship to help our students succeed academically and in the world beyond the classroom.
