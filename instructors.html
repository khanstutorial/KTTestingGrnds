New Updates

  • Everyone logs in through the same link now!

    Please click on "Instructor Login" on the left panel to log in.

  • Everyone's logins have also changed!

    The logins are now based on what is being taught and instructors no longer have personal logins. Please click here to access your new login information.

  • You should not change the password anymore!

    Other team members will be sharing the account with you moving forward so do not change the password.

  • If you get kicked out, you should be able to get back in just by joining the room again.

    We've resolved issues related to this bug... we think. We may not have, so let us know!

Important Information

  • Please do not record the lectures.

    That being said, we've upgraded everything so it should not be the end of the world if you do - but still don't. It has previously caused the server to fail.

  • Please disable the webcams for your students,

    but you can leave your webcam on. Running too many webcams increases CPU usage leading to slow connections and potential for server failure, so please don't do this.

  • If your internet drops while you are teaching a session, do not close the tab.

    Click the Retry Now button once you have internet again and you will be automatically reconnected with your session. If you get kicked out somehow or close the tab, first try to join the class again how you normally would start the classroom. If that still doesn't work, call us!

  • All students require moderator approval to join.

    In the beginning of the class, on the left hand side, there will be pop-up when students attempt to join and you must approve them to let them into the class. The second update is that all users will enter into the class with their mics off by default.

  • All students will have their mics off by default,

    so you no longer have to set that up.

  • We need your help to check this due to the potential for human error.

    Please login today well before your classes and ensure that you can access all of your classes for the weekend. There is a high potential for human error due to the fact that there are so many classes, so please check and let us know ahead of time so we're not scrambling a few minutes before your classes.
