Important Information

This is the first time many of us are doing something like this, and this is our first real-world run of this service. Don't worry, we've tested this a lot, but there are always variables we can't account for. We need y'all to do a few things for us:

  • Please do not record the lectures.

    There is a screenshot below of what that looks like. We don't have extensive storage space on our servers and it could crash the system.

  • Please disable the webcams for your students,

    but you can leave your webcam on. There's also screenshots below showing you how to do that. This is another server issue because running too many webcams increases CPU usage leading to slow connections and potential for server failure, so please don't do this.

  • If your internet drops while you are teaching a session, do not log out.

    Click the Retry Now button once you have internet connection and you will be automatically reconnected with your session.

  • New logins are now setup for all instructors.

    Please check the sheet to ensure that you know your login. All usernames have changed, along with all passwords. Please click here to access your new login information.

  • There are two new updates to the system by default that will affect the functionality.

    All users entering now require moderator approval. In the beginning of the class, on the left hand side, there will be pop-up when students attempt to join and you must approve them to let them into the class. The second update is that all users will enter into the class with their mics off by default so you no longer have to set that up.

  • If someone doesn't bookmark the link and needs to access their classes,

    you can give them this link: ktlearning.com/index.html

  • We need your help to check this due to the potential for human error.

    Please login today well before your classes and ensure that you can access all of your classes for the weekend. There is a high potential for human error due to the fact that there are so many classes, so please check and let us know ahead of time so we're not scrambling a few minutes before your classes.
